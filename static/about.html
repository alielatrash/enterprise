About MENA Daily Digest

Welcome to MENA Daily Digest, your intelligent news companion for staying informed about the Middle East & North Africa region without information overload.

What We Do

Every day, we automatically collect news from trusted sources across the MENA region and use advanced AI to create a concise, easy-to-read summary of the most important stories. Think of it as having a personal news assistant who reads hundreds of articles and tells you only what matters.

How It Works

  • Automatic Collection: We gather news from multiple trusted sources including Reuters, regional newspapers, and business publications
  • Smart Processing: Our AI analyzes each article, removes duplicates, and ranks stories by importance
  • AI Summarization: Using advanced language models, we create clear, concise summaries
  • Daily Delivery: Every morning at 8:30 AM Cairo time, your digest is ready
